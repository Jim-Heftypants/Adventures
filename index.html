The city state of Aarondel was once a peaceful land centered on commerce. It borders the Cobalt Ocean to the south, with rivers stemming in all directions from its center. Aarondel thrived through trade with the forest elves to the west and the dwarves and gnomes in the northern mountains.
Exiled from the Underdark, a mind-flayer took refuge within the city. As it grew in strength leeching on the city's populace, people began to disappear. Soon, King Etheric's wife, Queen Elthamira, went missing. Etheric used every available resource to search for her. While searching, the mind-flayer's hideout was discovered. Etheric sent in his royal gaurd and, after a desperate battle, the blight was killed.
Searching the cave revealed the source for the disappearances. Etheric saw his wife's lifeless body resting along the cave's back wall. In his despair, he swore to bring her back by any means. Etheric began researching the forbidden art of necromancy alone. For months he manically read and re-read every dark tome and scroll in the kingdom. Aarondel began to decline as he kept himself locked up in the palace study barely pausing even to eat and sleep.
Finally, he began the ritual to bring back the dead. Etheric opened a dark gateway in the palace room and summoned Elthamira's spirit through it. Her spirit returned to her body and after a few moments, she suddenly awoke. He was indescribably shocked and euphoric as they embraced.
Though his spell should have ended, the gateway remained open. Emerging from the abyss, a dark shadow surrounded them. This evil spirit flew into them and then suddenly disappeared, closing the portal with it. Wary, the King and Queen looked around to find everything was back to normal. Months passed.
They were now finding it hard to shut out the voices in their heads. The dark spirit, origionally just a whisper in the back of their minds, was gaining control over them. Memories became a blur. Days would pass in a trance. Times became unaccountable.
A year passed. King Etheric and Queen Elthamira scourged the land they loved, bringing everything they had built to ruin.
You represent a legendary band of Adventurers tasked by Etheric himself before his collapse to protect Aarondel against its greatest threats. Amongst your ranks are skilled fighters such as Paladins, Rangers, Wizards, and Clerics. Your choices will influence not only your own party but the very fate of Aarondel and its people. How will you respond to this evil?
